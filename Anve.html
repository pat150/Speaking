🗣️ Speak with Your AI Cambridge Examiner

This AI partner simulates authentic Cambridge Speaking exam tasks. It can act as an examiner for:

  • Part 1 — Personal questions and short answers
  • Part 2 — Individual long turn (1–2 minutes speaking on a topic)
  • Part 3 — Interactive discussion with follow-up questions
How to use it:
  • Click the microphone button that appears below
  • Allow microphone access in your browser
  • Say something like: “Let’s practise a C1 Part 3 discussion on the environment” or “Give me a B2 Part 2 topic about travel”
  • Speak naturally for 8–12 minutes
  • The AI will listen, respond with voice, and give feedback at the end

Recommended: Use headphones with a built-in microphone for the best experience.

After each session, note down 2–3 useful phrases or corrections and bring them to class.

Quick Start Prompts to Try

You can say these directly to the AI to begin a practice session:

  • “Start a B2 Speaking Part 2 long turn. Give me a topic about a memorable event.”
  • “Let’s do a C1 Part 3 discussion on the advantages and disadvantages of social media.”
  • “Act as a Cambridge examiner and ask me Part 1 questions about work and studies.”
  • “Give me feedback on my fluency after we finish this task.”
